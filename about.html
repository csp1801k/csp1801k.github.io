ACM ICPC

Students of the International IT University – the champions of Kazakhstan of the programming! In 2015 the Olympic team of IITU took the first place in 1/4 of the world championship of sport programming ACM ICPC 2015! ACM ICPC 2015-2016, NEERC, Kazakhstan Subregional Contest is the most prestigious programming competition in Kazakhstan, where our students take part every year. In 2014, among 122 universities, IITU was in the top-20 in the final of the programming world championship contest ACM ICPC 2014! .

ABU Robocon

Students of the International IT University take part in ABU ROBOCON contest. Robocon competition has been conducted since 2002 by Asia-Pacific broadcasting union (ABU). For the first time, on behalf of all Kazakhstan, students of the International IT University successfully presented development of robotics on the international level in Hong Kong in 2012. In 2015 in Indonesia, our team developed two robots, which demonstrated a real game of badminton: supply and reception.
