Information systems

Information systems is a field of science and technology, which includes a set of tools, techniques and methods of human activity aimed at the creation and application of systems of collection, reporting, storage, transmission and processing of information.

«5B070300 – Information Systems» specialty can carry out the following types of professional activities:
-design and engineering;
-production and processing;
–organization and management;
–operation.
Typical tasks of professional activity of the bachelor are:
– design and development of various information system components and information systems in general;
- installation, configuration and administration of the network infrastructure of information systems;
– design and administration of databases of information systems;
– information, software, technical, organizational and legal support of information systems and their elements.

About ITTU

The International Information Technology University>

«Within the FEZ framework favorable conditions are being created for productive development of higher educational institutions, including the International Information Technologies University, vocational educational institutions, research institutes» (Decree of the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan dated August 18, 2003 № 1166 «On Establishment of Information Technologies Park as a Special Economic Zone». The International Information Technology University was established in April 2009 on behalf of the President of RK (Resolution № 881 of the Government of RK dated 24 September, 2008.) for promoting the industrial-innovative development of Kazakhstan.
